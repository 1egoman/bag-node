How does it work?

Each user has a central "bag" that food items can be put into. Users can search for recipes created within the app and put those in their bag for centralized access. Bag will do all the magic of pulling in the price for each item and recipe, and with a few taps, will convert your bag's contents into a grocery list, sorted to match your grocery store's layout. To automate recipe discovery further, Bag will recommend recipes it thinks you'd like and give you a chance to decrease grocery-planning further. ("My Picks")
